Harley Davidson Touring CVO Road Glide

The Harley Davidson Street Glide ST combines the muscular Milwaukee-Eight 117 engine with new darkened and bronze finishes. The iconic Harley-Davidson batwing fairing features a split-air vent to limit head shake and a low-profile dark windshield. A lowered tank console and side filler cap and lowered engine guard add custom styling and differentiate it from other Touring models
